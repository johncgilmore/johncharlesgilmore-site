About John Charles Gilmore

I'm John Gilmore, a Fractional COO and startup builder with a bias toward clarity, acceleration, and radical operational honesty. After fifteen years leading operations in high-growth startups, I specialize in building systems that scale with the founder—not around them. My work spans finance, people, legal, product, and revenue ops—and I’m most useful when a company feels stuck and no one can say exactly why.

I'm driven by five core principles: Protect your ability to see, embody error correction, prioritize systems over sentiment, depend on others to sharpen your thinking, and accept that slow is fast. These values guide how I diagnose hidden bottlenecks and create execution systems that hold up under pressure.

If you’re building something fast—and it’s already outgrowing your team, systems, or clarity—I’ll help you catch up to your own vision.
